• Click in the above editing area (in the middle of the text), or even select part of it.
  • Click in the following input field to remove the focus from the editing area.
  • Click the "Focus" button to set the focus in the editing area again.


Expected results: The editing area must have the same selection (or caret position) that was defined when it lost the focus. (Works well on Firefox 1.5 and Opera 9)

Actual results: The editing area gets the focus, but the selection is lost, and the caret is placed in the beginning of the text.
